Start with one short paragraph explaining the product category, who it is for, and why it is worth examining now.

## Step 1 - Estimate the Total Addressable Market (TAM)

- What is the largest reasonable market for this category?
- Which adjacent market reports or assumptions are you using?
- What revenue size, buyer count, and growth rate matter most?

## Step 2 - Filter to Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)

Apply four filters:

1. Category share
2. Persona share
3. Platform share
4. Geography

Then explain what those filters suggest about the realistic buyer pool.

## Step 3 - Cross-Platform Research (CPR)

For each platform, capture:

- Buyer volume
- Competition level
- CCTS score
- Entry barrier
- Notes

## Step 4 - Compute SOM-C

Use the formula below and explain your assumptions:

`SOM-C = SOM x (1 - CCTS/100) x Differentiation Multiplier`

Then summarize which platform gives the cleanest route to attention.

## Step 5 - Determine Windows of Opportunity (WOO)

Describe:

- Window duration
- Why the window exists
- Entry barrier
- Risk level
- Recommended product

## Step 6 - Forecast Market Response (MR)

Sketch a realistic timeline:

- Month 1
- Months 2-3
- Months 4-6
- Month 12+

## Step 7 - Strategic Summary

Answer clearly:

1. Which product category should a new seller start with?
2. Why now?
3. What is the quickest path to first sales?
