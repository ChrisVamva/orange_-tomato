**SUBJECT:** MULTION

**STATUS:** ACTIVE

## LABOR DISPLACEMENT ANALYSIS
MultiOn operates at the highest echelon of autonomous consumer web automation, directly threatening human involvement in everyday digital logistics and e-commerce. It brings robust, API-driven web agents into the complex realm of consumer tasks—such as automated purchasing, booking, scheduling, and multi-platform data aggregation. By executing these tasks autonomously via a streamlined API-first interface or a browser extension, it functionally replaces personal assistants, administrative staff, and any role involving repetitive digital chores. The economic value of manual, human-driven web interaction is completely eviscerated by MultiOn’s capability to manage an entire user's digital life asynchronously, turning complex web flows into single API calls.

## TECHNICAL EVALUATION: TOMATO SCORE 6
A Tomato Score of 6 has been assigned to MultiOn. The rating reflects its critical operational value as a sophisticated consumer-facing agent, despite its reliance on a browser extension model which introduces distinct security and deployment considerations compared to isolated cloud environments. The score of 6 acknowledges its high degree of autonomy and its capacity to perform API-driven execution of highly complex web workflows. The technical achievement lies in its orchestration of multi-step, asynchronous actions across varied consumer platforms, proving its reliability in executing tasks that previously required deep human intuition and decision-making capabilities. MultiOn’s architecture is specifically tuned for high-fidelity execution of real-world logistics.

## LONG-TERM THREAT TO THE LEGACY DOM
MultiOn’s approach presents a significant threat to the legacy DOM, as it effectively treats the entire consumer web as a single, massive API. Its autonomous agents navigate the DOM dynamically, abstracting away the underlying structural complexities to fulfill the user's intent. For businesses relying on the friction of their web interfaces to control consumer behavior or limit automated interaction (such as ticket scalping, flash sales, or data scraping), MultiOn acts as a universal solvent. The legacy DOM's structural defenses are completely bypassed as MultiOn interprets the visual and semantic elements of the page directly, rendering traditional bot mitigation reliant on structural analysis entirely ineffective.
